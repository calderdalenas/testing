Fundraising

As a Branch we receive a small amount of money from the National Autistic Society, but most of our funding comes directly from our volunteers. Without regular fundraising we simply could not exist. If you can help in any way with fundraising, please do not hesitate to get in touch.

How we raise funds

The branch has regular fundraising events such as a Bric-a-Brac stall in Halifax and we also have stands in both Brighouse and Halifax Charity Galas, however in recent years both these events have struggled to keep going due to a lack of volunteers. Anyone who can help out with either selling tickets for these events or helping out on the day will be contributing to the future of the branch.

If you just want to come along and join the fun at any of our events, that's great too! Just use the fundraising category to find all the events here on the website.
