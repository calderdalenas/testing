Events

We try to organise regular events for our members to participate in. These are all for members and their families only so they are not publically advertised. You can keep in touch with what events we are hosting by either signing up to our mailing list or joining our facebook group.

There are other events around Calderdale which may also be of interest to people with Autism and their families.
