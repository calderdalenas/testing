About Calderdale NAS

Calderdale NAS is a local branch of the National Autistic Society. It is run entirely by volunteers with support from the NAS. If you would like to volunteer, you can read more about this on our volunteering page.

If you would like to sign up to our mailing list, please enter your email in the form below and click on the 'Subscribe' button. We send out an email about once a month, and if you want to unsubscribe it's very easy.

What we do

  • Raise funds
  • Signpost people to appropriate services
  • Organise Events
  • Feedback to the NAS
