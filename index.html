About Us

Calderdale NAS is a local branch of the National Autistic Society. It is run entirely by volunteers with support from the NAS. If you would like to volunteer, you can read more about this on our volunteering page.

History

In 2008, a group of parents and carers, who met informally to offer each other mutual support wanted to become a source of information and help to people with autism and their families across Calderdale. They decided this could be done more effectively by being part of the UK's leading charity for people with autism, and so the Calderdale branch of the National Autistic Society was formed.
