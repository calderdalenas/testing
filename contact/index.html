Contact Us

You can contact the branch by any of the methods list below. However if you need urgent support please contact the NAS hotlines listed.

To get in contact with the NAS Calderdale Branch email calderdale@nas.org.uk. We do have a branch phone but this is with a volunteer who will rarely be able to answer the phone but you can leave a message and they will endeavor to get back to you. The phone number is 07798 617448. If you need to speak to someone urgently the NAS helplines are listed below;
